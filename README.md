Nuvel is a Tunisian automotive startup concept founded by Skander Mansouri and Yessine, built around the vision of creating a new generation of vehicles designed for modern North African mobility. The idea behind Nuvel Cars is to combine European-style engineering standards with local Tunisian needs, focusing on affordability, durability, and smart technology integration. The founders aim to develop a brand that reflects Tunisia’s growing potential in the automotive and technology sectors, with a strong emphasis on innovation, clean design, and future electric mobility.

Nuvel Cars is imagined as a company that develops a range of vehicles including compact city cars, family sedans, and future electric models adapted to both urban roads and long-distance travel across Tunisia. The brand identity focuses on simplicity, aerodynamic design, and intelligent onboard systems such as digital dashboards, connectivity features, and safety assistance. The long-term vision includes building local assembly partnerships, creating job opportunities in Tunisia, and gradually expanding into export markets across Africa and the Mediterranean region.

In terms of presence and communication, Nuvel Cars is structured as a developing startup project where customers, partners, and investors can connect through official communication channels such as email and phone support (to be officially confirmed as the project develops). The founders, Skander and Yessine, position Nuvel as more than just a car brand—it is a long-term industrial project aimed at contributing to Tunisia’s automotive future, encouraging local innovation, and building a competitive name in the global mobility industry.
